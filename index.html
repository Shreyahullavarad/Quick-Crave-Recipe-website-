Butter Garlic pasta

About Meal

Butter Garlic Pasta is a quick and flavorful dish made with spaghetti tossed in butter, garlic, herbs, and chili flakes. It’s a simple Italian comfort meal perfect for busy days, ready in just 15 minutes. You can also add cheese or veggies to make it even richer and heartier.

Ingridients:


200g spaghetti or pasta of choice
3 tbsp butter
6–7 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
1 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
1/2 tsp mixed herbs or oregano
Salt (to taste)
1/4 tsp black pepper
Grated parmesan or processed cheese (for garnish)
Fresh parsley or coriander (for garnish)

Instruction:


Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and keep aside.
Heat butter and olive oil in a pan over medium flame.
Add chopped garlic and sauté until golden and fragrant.
Sprinkle chili flakes and herbs, mix for a few seconds.
Add the boiled pasta and toss well to coat with butter and garlic.
Season with salt and black pepper.
Turn off heat and sprinkle cheese and fresh parsley.
Serve hot and enjoy your quick Butter Garlic Pasta!

Masala Omelette

About Meal

Masala Omelette is a spicy Indian twist on the classic omelette — made with whisked eggs, onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and flavorful spices. It’s a quick, protein-rich breakfast or snack that’s ready in minutes and full of taste and texture.

Ingridients:


2 eggs
2 tbsp finely chopped onion
1 small tomato, finely chopped
1 green chili, finely chopped
2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1/4 tsp red chili powder
Salt to taste
1 tbsp milk (optional, for fluffiness)
1 tsp butter or oil for frying

Instruction:


Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them well.
Add onions, tomatoes, green chili, coriander leaves, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and milk.
Whisk everything together until nicely combined.
Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.
Cook for 2–3 minutes on one side until golden brown.
Flip and cook the other side for another minute.
Serve hot with toast, chutney, or ketchup.

Veggie Sandwich

About Meal

Veggie Sandwich is a quick and healthy snack packed with fresh vegetables, creamy spreads, and soft bread. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a light meal, it’s colorful, crunchy, and satisfying — ready in just a few minutes!

Ingridients:


4 slices of bread (white, brown, or multigrain)
2 tbsp butter or mayonnaise
1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
1 small tomato, thinly sliced
1 small onion, thinly sliced
1 boiled potato, sliced
4–5 lettuce leaves
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 tsp chaat masala (optional)
Cheese slice (optional)

Instruction:


Spread butter or mayonnaise on all slices of bread.
Place lettuce on the bottom slice.
Add layers of cucumber, tomato, onion, and potato slices.
Sprinkle salt, pepper, and chaat masala.
Add a cheese slice if you like.
Cover with another slice of bread.
Grill in a sandwich maker or toast on a pan until golden brown.
Cut diagonally and serve with green chutney or ketchup.

Paneer Stir Fry

About Meal

Paneer Stir Fry is a quick and flavorful Indian dish made with soft paneer cubes sautéed with colorful bell peppers, onions, and aromatic spices. It’s perfect as a light meal, snack, or side dish — full of protein and ready in under 15 minutes!

Ingridients:


200g paneer (cut into cubes)
1 small onion, sliced
1 small green bell pepper, sliced
1 small red bell pepper, sliced
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp tomato ketchup
1/2 tsp chili sauce (optional)
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
1 tbsp oil
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instruction:


Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
Add onions and bell peppers; stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
Add tomato ketchup, soy sauce, and chili sauce.
Gently add the paneer cubes and toss to coat evenly.
Cook for another 2–3 minutes, stirring lightly.
Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.
Serve hot with roti, rice, or as a spicy appetizer.

Aloo Tikki Burger

About Meal

Aloo Tikki Burger is a delicious Indian-style burger featuring a crispy potato patty sandwiched between soft burger buns with lettuce, onion, tomato, and flavorful sauces. It’s a perfect blend of Indian spices and western comfort — quick, crunchy, and satisfying!

Ingridients:


2 burger buns
2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
2 tbsp bread crumbs
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp cumin powder
Salt to taste
1 tbsp cornflour (mixed with 2 tbsp water)
Oil for shallow frying
1 tbsp butter
2 lettuce leaves
1 small onion, sliced
1 small tomato, sliced
2 tbsp mayonnaise or burger sauce
2 cheese slices (optional)

Instruction:


In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, bread crumbs, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, cumin, and salt.
Form the mixture into two round patties.
Dip each patty in cornflour mixture and coat lightly with bread crumbs if desired.
Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the patties until golden and crisp on both sides.
Slice the burger buns in half and toast them with butter on a pan.
Spread mayonnaise or burger sauce on the buns.
Place lettuce on the bottom bun, then the tikki, followed by tomato, onion, and cheese slice.
Cover with the top bun and press gently.
Serve hot with fries and ketchup!

Egg Fried Rice

About Meal

Egg Fried Rice is a quick and flavorful Asian-inspired dish made with cooked rice, scrambled eggs, vegetables, and soy sauce. It’s a perfect one-pan meal — simple, tasty, and ready in under 15 minutes!

Ingridients:


2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
2 eggs
1 small onion, finely chopped
1/4 cup carrot, finely chopped
1/4 cup capsicum, finely chopped
1/4 cup spring onions, chopped
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp vinegar (optional)
1/2 tsp black pepper
Salt to taste
1 tbsp oil

Instruction:


Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
Add the chopped onion, carrot, and capsicum. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
Push the veggies to one side of the pan.
Crack the eggs on the other side and scramble until cooked.
Mix the scrambled eggs with the veggies.
Add cooked rice, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
Stir-fry everything for 3–4 minutes, ensuring the rice is evenly coated.
Add chopped spring onions, mix, and cook for another minute.
Serve hot with chili sauce or enjoy as is!

Lemon Rice

About Meal

Lemon Rice is a refreshing and tangy South Indian rice dish made with cooked rice, lemon juice, and a flavorful tempering of mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts. It’s light, aromatic, and perfect for lunch or travel meals.

Ingridients:


2 cups cooked rice
2 tbsp oil
1 tsp mustard seeds
1 tsp urad dal (optional)
1 tsp chana dal (optional)
2 green chilies, slit
1 dry red chili (optional)
8–10 curry leaves
1/4 tsp turmeric powder
2 tbsp roasted peanuts or cashews
Juice of 1 large lemon
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instruction:


Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
Add urad dal, chana dal, and fry until golden.
Add green chilies, red chili, curry leaves, and peanuts. Sauté for a minute.
Add turmeric powder and mix well.
Turn off the flame and add lemon juice and salt.
Now add the cooked rice and gently mix until evenly coated.
Garnish with coriander leaves.
Serve warm with curd, papad, or pickle!

Fruit Yogurt Bowl

About Meal

Fruit Yogurt Bowl is a colorful and healthy breakfast or snack made with creamy yogurt, fresh fruits, and crunchy toppings. It’s light, energizing, and full of natural sweetness — perfect for a quick, nutritious start to your day!

Ingridients:


1 cup thick yogurt or Greek yogurt
1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
1/2 cup banana slices
1/2 cup apple cubes
1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
2 tbsp granola or oats
1 tbsp chia seeds or flax seeds (optional)
1 tbsp dry fruits or nuts (almonds, walnuts, or cashews)
Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

Instruction:


In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
Add honey or maple syrup and mix well.
Pour the yogurt into a serving bowl.
Arrange the fruits neatly on top (banana, apple, berries).
Sprinkle granola, seeds, and chopped nuts over the top.
Dust with a pinch of cinnamon if you like.
Serve chilled immediately and enjoy a fresh, fruity delight!

Tandoori Paneer Wrap

About Meal

Tandoori Paneer Wrap is a delicious fusion dish made with spicy marinated paneer, fresh veggies, and creamy sauces all wrapped in a soft roti or tortilla. It’s smoky, tangy, and absolutely perfect for lunch or on-the-go meals!

Ingridients:


200g paneer (cut into strips)
1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
1/2 tsp cumin powder
1 tsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
1 tbsp oil
4 rotis or tortillas
1 small onion, sliced
1 small capsicum, sliced
2 tbsp mayonnaise or mint chutney
2 tbsp yogurt sauce or hung curd (optional)
Butter for toasting

Instruction:


In a bowl, mix curd, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, lemon juice, and salt.
Add paneer pieces and coat them well with the marinade.
Let it rest for 15–20 minutes.
Heat oil in a pan and sauté the marinated paneer until lightly charred and cooked.
Warm the rotis or tortillas on a tawa with a little butter.
Spread mayonnaise or mint chutney on the roti.
Place a few pieces of tandoori paneer along with sliced onions and capsicum.
Drizzle yogurt sauce if desired and roll tightly.
Toast the wrap on the pan until golden and crisp.
Cut in half and serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.

Chocolate Mug Cake

About Meal

Chocolate Mug Cake is a quick, single-serving dessert made in a mug and ready in just 2 minutes! Soft, gooey, and rich in chocolate flavor — it’s the perfect instant treat for late-night cravings or a sweet pick-me-up.

Ingridients:


4 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
2 tbsp cocoa powder
3 tbsp sugar
1/4 tsp baking powder
A pinch of salt
3 tbsp milk
2 tbsp oil or melted butter
1/4 tsp vanilla essence
1 tbsp chocolate chips (optional)

Instruction:


In a microwave-safe mug, add flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
Add milk, oil, and vanilla essence. Stir until smooth and lump-free.
Add chocolate chips on top or mix them in.
Microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds (time may vary by microwave).
Let it cool for a minute before eating.
Top with ice cream, chocolate syrup, or nuts for extra indulgence.
Enjoy your instant homemade dessert!

Cheesy Maggi

About Meal

Cheesy Maggi is a creamy, indulgent twist on the classic Maggi noodles — loaded with melted cheese, spices, and veggies for extra flavor. It’s the ultimate quick comfort meal that’s ready in just 10 minutes!

Ingridients:


1 packet Maggi noodles
1 cup water
1/4 cup milk
1 tbsp butter
1/4 cup grated cheese (mozzarella or processed)
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped capsicum
1/4 cup boiled corn (optional)
1 Maggi tastemaker sachet
1/4 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
1/4 tsp black pepper powder
Salt to taste

Instruction:


Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions, capsicum, and corn for 2–3 minutes.
Add 1 cup water and the Maggi noodles.
Sprinkle the tastemaker and cook until the noodles soften.
Pour in milk and mix well for a creamy texture.
Add grated cheese and stir until melted and smooth.
Season with black pepper and chili flakes.
Cook for another minute until everything blends nicely.
Serve hot and enjoy your cheesy, gooey Maggi delight!
